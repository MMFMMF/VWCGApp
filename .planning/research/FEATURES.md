# Features Research

## Executive Summary

The 11-tool spec covers strong fundamentals but has gaps in competitive analysis, financial metrics, and ongoing engagement features. The synthesis engine concept is differentiating, but the current 5 rules are too narrow. Key findings:

**Strengths:**
- AI Readiness Assessment aligns with 2026 SMB priorities (59% adoption rate)
- Leadership DNA and Business EQ are uncommon in free tools (differentiating)
- 90-Day Roadmap matches standard SMB planning cycles
- Report Center enables lead capture through value exchange

**Weaknesses:**
- Vision Canvas overlaps heavily with standard Business Model Canvas (table stakes)
- SOP tools (3 of 11 features) may be overweighted for initial assessment phase
- Missing financial readiness and competitive positioning assessments
- No goal tracking or progress measurement beyond roadmap creation
- Synthesis rules focus heavily on gaps/problems vs. opportunity identification

**Strategic Recommendation:**
Keep core assessment tools (1-5), consolidate SOP features into one module (6-8), add financial readiness and competitive analysis, enhance synthesis engine with 3-5 additional opportunity-focused rules.

## Synthesis Rules Assessment

Current spec has 5 rules (E1-E5). Assessment:

### Current Rules Analysis

**E1: Execution Capability Gap**
- **Status:** âœ… KEEP
- **Logic:** AI Readiness.score < 4 AND Leadership DNA.execution < Leadership DNA.target.execution
- **Value:** Identifies readiness to implement despite vision
- **Improvement:** Add threshold severity levels (minor/moderate/critical)

**E2: Unmitigated Threat**
- **Status:** âœ… KEEP
- **Logic:** SWOT has threat with confidence > 0.7 AND no corresponding strength/opportunity to counter it
- **Value:** Highlights strategic blind spots
- **Improvement:** Good as-is, consider linking to recommended mitigation strategies

**E3: Burnout Risk**
- **Status:** âœ… KEEP
- **Logic:** Business EQ trending down for 2+ periods AND Leadership DNA shows high gap
- **Value:** Personal/health dimension often overlooked
- **Improvement:** Add threshold for severity (warning vs. critical)

**E4: Strength Leverage (broken in original)**
- **Status:** âš ï¸ FIX & KEEP
- **Logic:** Should identify under-leveraged strengths (high confidence strength NOT connected to opportunities)
- **Value:** Identifies missed opportunities
- **Fix Needed:** Clarify logic - looks for strengths in SWOT that don't connect to value proposition or revenue strategy

**E5: SOP Metric Missing**
- **Status:** âš ï¸ RECONSIDER if SOP tools consolidated
- **Logic:** Vision Canvas has metrics/KPIs but no SOP exists to track them
- **Value:** Operational gap identification
- **Alternative:** If SOPs consolidated to maturity assessment, change to "Process-Metric Misalignment" (metrics defined but process maturity too low to track reliably)

### Recommended Additional Rules

**E6: Financial-Growth Misalignment** â—
- **Logic:** 90-Day Roadmap has aggressive growth initiatives BUT Financial Readiness score < 6/10
- **Value:** Prevents overextension, cash flow crises
- **Output:** "Your roadmap requires investment, but financial runway is limited. Prioritize cash-positive initiatives first."
- **Dependencies:** Requires Financial Readiness Assessment (missing feature #1)

**E7: Competitive Blind Spot** â—
- **Logic:** Competitive Position shows 2+ competitors with superior offering in key dimension AND SWOT doesn't list as threat
- **Value:** Ensures SWOT accuracy, market realism
- **Output:** "Competitive analysis reveals threats not captured in your SWOT. Consider updating."
- **Dependencies:** Requires Competitive Positioning Assessment (missing feature #2)

**E8: Market-Product Disconnect**
- **Logic:** Customer Validation score < 3/5 BUT Vision Canvas shows aggressive market expansion plans
- **Value:** Prevents premature scaling
- **Output:** "Before expanding, validate product-market fit with current customers."
- **Dependencies:** Requires Customer/Market Validation Assessment (missing feature #5)

**E9: Leadership-Team Capability Gap**
- **Logic:** Leadership DNA shows strong scores BUT Team Alignment score < 5/10
- **Value:** Identifies execution risk (leader can't execute alone)
- **Output:** "Your leadership is strong, but team alignment is weak. Focus on delegation and communication."
- **Dependencies:** Requires Team Alignment Assessment (missing feature #4)

**E10: Opportunity-Capability Match** â­
- **Logic:** SWOT has high-confidence opportunity (>0.7) AND AI/Financial/Leadership scores align to execute it
- **Value:** POSITIVE synthesis rule (not gap-focused)
- **Output:** "You have a strong opportunity in [X] and the capabilities to pursue it. Prioritize this in your roadmap."
- **Note:** This is critical - current rules are ALL gap-focused. Need some positive reinforcement.

**E11: Strength Multiplication**
- **Logic:** Leadership DNA strength + corresponding AI Readiness high score + relevant SWOT strength
- **Value:** Identifies "compounding advantages"
- **Output:** "Your strength in [X] is reinforced across multiple dimensions. This is a core competitive advantage to lean into."
- **Note:** Another positive/opportunity rule

### Synthesis Engine Summary

**Current:** 5 rules (4 problem-focused, 1 broken)
**Recommended:** 11 rules
- **Keep/Fix:** E1, E2, E3, E4 (fixed), E5 (modified)
- **Add (Problem-Focused):** E6 Financial-Growth Misalignment, E7 Competitive Blind Spot, E8 Market-Product Disconnect, E9 Leadership-Team Gap
- **Add (Opportunity-Focused):** E10 Opportunity-Capability Match, E11 Strength Multiplication

**Key Change:** Balance problem identification with opportunity identification (currently too skewed toward gaps)
